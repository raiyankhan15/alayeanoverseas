Our Story

Built on a promise
of purity.

"We don't export just commodities. We export the result of generations of craft, knowledge, and care."

Alayean Overseas was founded with a single conviction: that the world deserves access to the finest agro produce South Asia has to offer — traceable, certified, and delivered with care.

We partner directly with family farms and co-operatives across India, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, and beyond, cutting out the layers that compromise quality and trust. Every shipment carries our name — and that means everything.

How It Works

Farm to freight,
no shortcuts.

01 🌱 Origin Sourcing

We identify and partner with farms and co-operatives that meet our quality criteria — personally verified, long-term relationships.

02 🔬 Quality Grading

Every batch is independently tested for purity, moisture content, and contamination before being accepted for export.

03 📦 Pack & Document

Professional packaging, full phytosanitary certification, FSSAI compliance, and customs clearance handled end-to-end.

04 ✈️ Global Delivery

Air and sea freight to over 12 countries. Real-time tracking, dedicated account contact, and no-surprise delivery.
